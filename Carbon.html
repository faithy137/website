What is Carbon?

A nonmetallic element found more or less pure in nature (as in diamond and graphite) or as a part of coal and petroleum and of the bodies of living things or obtained artificially see element.

Why is it Important?

It regulate the Earth's temperature, make up the food that sustains us, and provide energy that fuels our global economy.

Where to find it?

Carbon is found in all living things.
