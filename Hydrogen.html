What is Hydrogen?

Hydrogen is a clean alternative to methane, also known as natural gas. It's the most abundant chemical element, estimated to contribute 75% of the mass of the universe.

Why is it Important?
Hydrogen is an essential for life, the universe and just about everything. Life, in fact, is multiply dependent on it. Without hydrogen we wouldn't have the Sun to give us heat and light. There would be no useful organic compounds to form the building blocks of life.

Where to find it? The good news is hydrogen is found almost everywhere.

  • Water
  • Plants
  • Rocks
  • Humans
  • Animals

    • How do we use it?
    Hydrogen can be used to power vehicles, generate electricity, power industry and heat our homes and businesses.
